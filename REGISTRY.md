# Registry Descriptions

## npm

French government cybersecurity standards MCP -- ANSSI RGS, ANSSI Guide d'hygiene informatique, SecNumCloud, PGSSI-S, CNIL Guide securite, HDS. Part of the Ansvar MCP Network (ansvar.ai/mcp).

## Smithery

Access French government cybersecurity standards including ANSSI RGS, SecNumCloud, PGSSI-S (healthcare), CNIL data protection guide, and HDS certification. Bilingual (French + English) with FTS search, ISO 27002 cross-references, and sector-based filtering.

## Glama

French government cybersecurity standards MCP covering 6 frameworks and 400+ controls. Search by keyword, sector, or ISO mapping. Bilingual French/English support.
